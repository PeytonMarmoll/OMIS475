CD Album Releases
Songs about Jane
Songs about Jane is an album dedicated to love and all of the girls the band has loved before. It describes heartbreak, troubles and new beginnings. I liked this album because it was very relatable and something you can cry too.
Red Pill Blues
Red Blue Pills is an album dedicated to a new sound of soft rock. It features leading rappers in today's music culture. I liked this album because it pumps you up getting you excited and it's something you can play to uplift yourself.
Overexposed
This album is an alternative rock album with more classical features like Christina Agulera. I loved the casual music this album provided, it was music you can relax to and play for every occasion.
